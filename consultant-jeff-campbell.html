Jeffery Campbell

Agile coach & project manager

Jeff is an Agile Coach who considers the discovery of Agile and Lean to be one of the most defining moments of his life, and considers helping others to improve their working life not to simply be a job, but a social responsibility. As an Agile Coach, he has worked with driving Agile transformations in organisations both small and large.

He is also involved in the Agile community outside of the work place. He is one of the founding members of Gothenburg's largest Agile community, at 300+ members www.scrumbeers.com, a community run Agile discussion group. He also organises the annual conference www.brewingagile.org.

Having worked with Jeff for more than three years on numerous projects, I know exactly what he would contribute - great project management, clear communication with team and management, enthusiasm that he would make sure to transfer to his team, great problem solving when things got tough, useful technical know-how, a lot of new ideas and a lot of laughs.

Mattias Lundmark - Program Manager VAS at Tieto Healthcare & Welfare
