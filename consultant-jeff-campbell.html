Overview

I am a Agile Coach who considers the discovery of Agile and Lean to be one of the most defining moments of my life, and consider helping others to improve their working life not to simply be a job, but a social responsibility. As an Agile Coach, I have worked with driving Agile transformations in organisations both small and large.

I am heavily also involved in the Agile community outside of the work place as one of the founding members of Gothenburg's largest Agile community, at 300+ members www.scrumbeers.com, a community run Agile discussion group. I also organises the annual conference www.brewingagile.org.

Services

While difficult to perform, my role is easy to describe. I help individuals, teams, and organisation to improve themselves.

My favourite way to achieve this is to get my hands dirty, working on a daily basis with organisations who build and delivery products and services. Working to help teams understand and apply Agile and Lean principles, practices, and tools on a continuous basis.

However, I also provide introductions in the form on courses and workshops to help build enthusiasm, and provide your organisation with the basic toolset to get them started on their journey towards Agility.
